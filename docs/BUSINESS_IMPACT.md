## Impact Calculations

### Assumptions

- Target users (Year 1): 100,000 farmers
- Average farm size: 1.5 hectares
- Crops per year: 2 seasons
- Average crop value: ₹50,000/hectare/season

### 1. Time Saved

**Before AgriBloom:**
- Travel to KVK: 2-4 hours
- Wait for diagnosis: 1-3 days
- Return visit for report: 2-4 hours
- **Total: 3-7 days**

**With AgriBloom:**
- Upload photo: 1 minute
- Get diagnosis: 30 seconds
- **Total: 2 minutes**

**Time Saved per Query:** ~3-7 days → **99% reduction**

**Annual Impact (100K farmers, 4 queries/year):**
```
400,000 queries × 4 hours saved = 1,600,000 hours saved
= 200,000 working days saved annually
```

---

### 2. Cost Reduced

**Travel Cost Saved:**
```
100,000 farmers × 4 trips/year × ₹300/trip = ₹12 Crore/year
```

**Diagnostic Service Cost Saved:**
```
100,000 farmers × 2 paid consultations × ₹200 = ₹4 Crore/year
```

**Total Cost Saved: ₹16 Crore/year** (for 100K users)

---

### 3. Revenue Recovered (Yield Loss Prevention)

**Yield Loss Without Intervention:**
- Average loss: 25% of crop value
- Per farmer: 1.5 ha × 2 seasons × ₹50,000 × 25% = ₹37,500/year lost

**With Early Detection (AgriBloom):**
- Reduced loss: 10% (catching disease 5-7 days earlier)
- Per farmer: 1.5 ha × 2 seasons × ₹50,000 × 10% = ₹15,000/year lost

**Revenue Recovered per Farmer: ₹22,500/year**

**Annual Impact (100K farmers):**
```
100,000 × ₹22,500 = ₹225 Crore recovered annually
```
